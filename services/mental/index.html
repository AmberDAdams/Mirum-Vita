Life Coaching Services

At Mirum Vita, we know that mental troubles can be just as difficult as physical pains. We are here to help.

Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT)

Our emotions and thoughts affect our behaviour. Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT) is a tool used to help us understand why we feel the way we do, and to help us address any negative behaviour. Remember this: it is not the situation that matters; it is how you deal with it! CBT will assist you in politely getting your view across, without feeling you should pay penance for it - whether you are being bullied or harassed at work, are the bully yourself and cannot help “schadenfreude”, or are finding yourself unable to speak up to someone in your family circle.

Neuro-Linguistic Programming (NLP)

Often, we have self-limiting beliefs which prevent us from achieving our goals in life. Neuro-Linguistic Programming (NLP) is an extremely useful tool to promote one’s self-confidence and sense of worth, which builds your mindset to, for example, begin diet or lifestyle changes. I can attest to the success of this therapy from experience with clients. In addition, as somebody who has truly been through the mill, I can also attest that it has helped me personally!

Under the umbrella of forensic coaching, both CBT and NLP will help you to defeat your demons and move forward with your life. Together we can get you on a positive path towards your goals and dreams. If these treatments sound right for you, please submit a booking request here.
